About Runpik

AI product photography

Runpik turns a simple phone photo of your product into a professional, studio-quality photograph — in seconds, without a photographer, a studio, or expensive equipment.

What Runpik does

You upload a photo of a real, physical product — a piece of clothing, a cake, a bottle, a gadget, a piece of furniture. Runpik's AI produces a clean, professional shot of the same object — same shape, same colors, same logos and text — placed in a photographic context you choose: white studio, lifestyle scene, serving shot, and more. If the result isn't quite right, you refine it with simple text instructions until it is.

Who it's for

Runpik is built for anyone who sells physical products and needs great photos without a photo shoot:

  • Marketplace sellers (Wildberries, Ozon, Amazon, Etsy, and others).
  • Small businesses, makers, and independent brands.
  • Restaurants, cafés, and food producers.
  • Online stores and anyone building a product catalogue.

If you can take a photo with your phone, you can get studio-quality product images.

How it works

  • 1. Upload. Snap or upload a photo of your product.
  • 2. Analyze. Runpik recognizes the product and recommends the best type of shot.
  • 3. Generate. Pick a style and get a professional photograph of the same product.
  • 4. Improve. Not perfect? Refine it with a text prompt — "warmer background", "remove the shadow on the left" — and get a new version.

Your photos stay yours

We do not use your photos to train AI models, we do not sell them, and we do not publish them. Your content is processed only to create your images, and it is deleted when you remove it or close your account. See our Privacy Policy for the details.

Built independently

Runpik is built and operated by an independent developer based in the Republic of Armenia. It's an early-stage product that's growing fast — and your feedback directly shapes what comes next.
